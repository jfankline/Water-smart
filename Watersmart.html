Key Features

Farmer Registration

Simple sign-up with phone number for SMS alerts, farm location details, and secure login options.

Rainwater Tracking

Monitor tanks, ponds, or cisterns with capacity and current fill levels. Integrates with weather APIs.

Leak Detection

AI-powered anomaly detection spots abnormal water usage patterns and sends instant alerts.

Water Budgeting

Calculate safe daily usage targets during drought periods with "days-of-water-remaining" forecasts.

Offline-First Design

Progressive Web App works without constant internet, syncing data when connection is available.

Community Insights

Leaderboards and maps show collective water savings and leaks prevented across communities.
